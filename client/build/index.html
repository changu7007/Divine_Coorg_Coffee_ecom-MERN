Our instant coffee powders come in three delicious flavors: hazelnut, chocoorange, and cardamom.
Our filter coffee comes in two distinct flavors: Gold and Exotic. Gold has a rich, bold flavor, while Exotic is a more complex blend with hints of fruit and chocolate.
We don't provide a return procedure for our coffee products. We believe that coffee is a perishable item that can't be resold once it has left our warehouse. However, if you're not satisfied with your purchase, please email us at divinecoorgcoffe@yahoo.com and we'll do our best to make it right.
Our coffee is freshly roasted and packaged, so it's best to consume it within 2-3 months of purchase. We recommend storing your coffee in an airtight container in a cool, dry place to help preserve its flavor and freshness.
